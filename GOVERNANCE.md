## Committee Structure

### 1. Architecture Review Committee
- **Chair**: CTO
- **Members**: Atlas (CSO), CQO, CRO
- **Function**: Approves all architecture changes before engineering
- **Meets**: Per major version (v0.x → v1.0)

### 2. Risk Committee
- **Chair**: CRO
- **Members**: Valentine (CEO), CQO, Atlas (CSO)
- **Function**: Sets risk limits, approves go-live, reviews drawdown
- **Meets**: Weekly during live trading, monthly during demo

### 3. Research Committee
- **Chair**: Atlas (CSO)
- **Members**: CQO, CTO
- **Function**: Approves research direction, allocates compute budget
- **Meets**: Bi-weekly

### 4. Quality & Security Committee
- **Chair**: CTO
- **Members**: CRO, Atlas (CSO)
- **Function**: Code review gate, security audit, dependency scan
- **Meets**: Per commit (automated) + quarterly (manual)
